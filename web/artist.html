ABOUT THE ARTIST

JAY CHOU

BIO

Jay Chou (b. Jan 18, 1979) is a Taiwanese singer, songwriter, producer, actor, and director. Debuting in 2000, he became a major figure in Mandopop, known for his unique style and songwriting. Often called the “King of Asian Pop,” he has been recognized by TIME and CNN for his influence in Asia.

Vocals:

Mandarin Pop, R&B, Hip-Hop

Skills:

Songwriting, Composing, Arranging, Producing

Pushing Music,Beyond Boundaries
